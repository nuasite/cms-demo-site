## Welcome

Setting up a home spa is easier than you think. This guide walks through the
**three key decisions**: location, power, and water care.

1. Pick a level, well-drained spot.
2. Plan the electrical supply early.
3. Decide on a water-care routine.

> Tip: measure your access route before ordering.
